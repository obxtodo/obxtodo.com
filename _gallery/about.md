### About OBX To Do
Outer Banks Family Fun: Your family-first guide to the best things to do in the Outer Banks, from lighthouses to local live music.

### The Vision
OBX To Do started as a simple pursuit to stay connected to the Outer Banks, but it quickly evolved into the ultimate guide for things to do in the Outer Banks. Whether checking off bucket-list climbs at the lighthouses, chasing sunsets at Jockey’s Ridge, or catching live music over the waves at Fish Heads, we’re here to spotlight the local events and hidden gems that make the Outer Banks feel like home.

### Our Story
We are a Maryland-based family but our hearts live in the OBX. With kids in tow, we’ve spent years discovering the heartbeat of the islands. OBX To Do is our way of sharing those family-first attractions and events with you.

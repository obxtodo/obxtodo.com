### The Vision
"obxtodo" started as a way to organize our family trips to the Outer Banks, but it quickly grew into a digital gallery for anyone who loves the NC coast. Whether it's the climb up Cape Hatteras or a quiet sunset in Corolla, we want to capture the "heartbeat" of the islands.

### Our Story
We are a Maryland-based family with a deep-rooted love for the OBX. With three kids in tow, our guides are designed with families in mind—focusing on fun attractions and events.

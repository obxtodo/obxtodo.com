### The Vision
"obxtodo" started as a simple way to organize our own family adventures, but it quickly grew into a mission to capture the true "heartbeat" of the islands. Whether checking off bucket-list climbs at the lighthouses, chasing sunsets at Jockey’s Ridge, or catching live music over the waves at Fish Heads, we’re here to spotlight the local events and hidden gems that make the Outer Banks feel like home.

### Our Story
We are a Maryland-based family but our hearts live in the OBX. With three kids in tow, we’ve spent years discovering the heartbeat of the islands. obxtodo is our way of sharing those family-first attractions and events with you.
